Bougainvillea

Bougainvillea likes a place with plenty of sunshine and good drainage. Darkness and moisture will cause the branches to become elongated and difficult to bloom. Do not apply too much nitrogen fertilizer, otherwise it will only grow leaves but not bloom. In late spring and early summer, it is advisable to water less so that the edges are almost withered and pruned. Branches can promote flowering.

Four Seasons Begonia

Begonias in all seasons can be divided into dwarf, neutral and tall varieties according to the height of the plant; and according to their shape, they can be divided into upright and prostrate. Upright varieties have fewer branches. It is a low-growing perennial herb with a height of about 10-15 cm.

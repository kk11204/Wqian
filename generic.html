in blooming season

The landscape garden, planted and designed to look like a forest, has become a favorite check-in scene for Internet beauties.

Bougainvillea has been introduced to Asia for a long time. It is generally used in hedges, potted plants, garden flowers, flower corridors, flower walls, shade sheds and other landscaping purposes. This flower likes a high-temperature environment. It can bloom luxuriantly in a place with sufficient sunshine and good drainage. If it is in the shade, the branches will become elongated and it will be difficult to bloom. It grows quickly and is easy to cultivate. Its branches are also quite tough and resistant to pruning. You can often see people cutting it into various shapes such as animals and clouds. There are also quite a few improved varieties. The early varieties were mainly suitable for potted plants in large courtyards. Later, small potted plants also appeared. Bougainvillea has its peak growth season in the summer, when it will grow long branches. Since the long branches will consume a lot of nutrients from the bougainvillea and destroy the original plant shape, they should be cut off quickly. The general method of promoting flowering is to create an environment of alternating dry and wet conditions, which can promote large blooming of bougainvillea. The original variety has thorns on the young stems, but there are also varieties that have been improved to be thornless. The flower colors are even more colorful. In addition to red, pink, white, purple, orange, yellow and other colors, there are also two-color or multi-color varieties on the market.
